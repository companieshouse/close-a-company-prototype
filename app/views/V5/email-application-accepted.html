The process to close the company has now started.

Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors or members must send a copy to all interested parties, within 7 days of making the application. To do this, they'll need to:

  • search for the company on the Companies House public register
  • select ‘Filing history’
  • download the PDF document labelled ‘Application to strike the company off the register’

Search the Companies House public register: https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/

Read the full list of interested parties to tell about the company closing: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/striking-off-or-dissolving-a-limited-company

What happens next

We'll publish a strike off notice for the company in the relevant Gazette. After 2 months, unless there is an objection to the company closing, another notice will be published in the relevant Gazette and the company will be dissolved.
