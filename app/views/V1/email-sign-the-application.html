An application has been started to strike off and dissolve GAIL BIDDULPH LIMITED.

It must be signed by more than half of the directors before it can be submitted.

You will need to sign the application before 30 June 2025.

To do this, you will need:

  • to sign in or create your own Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code

Sign the application: https://staging.company-information.service.gov.uk/close-a-company/search-company

After you have signed the application

Once all signatures have been received, the person making the application can pay for and submit it. They should notify you once this has been done.

Directors must then send a signed copy of the application to all interested parties within 7 days. You will be able to download this by signing back in to the service using the link above.

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to close a company

Read the full list of who to tell about the strike off application: https://www.gov.uk/strike-off-your-company-from-companies-register/apply-to-strike-off
