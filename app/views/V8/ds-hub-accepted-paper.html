We received a paper application to close this company. We've accepted the application, and the process to close the company has started.

Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors must send a copy of this application to all interested parties within 7 days of making the application.

Download a copy of the application from the Companies House public register. You'll need to view the PDF document labelled 'Application to strike the company off the register'.

Read the full list of interested parties to tell about the company closing (opens in new tab).

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to close a company.

What happens next

We'll publish a strike off notice for the company in the relevant Gazette. After 2 months, unless there is an objection to the company closing, another notice will be published in the relevant Gazette and the company will be dissolved.
