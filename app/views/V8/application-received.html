We received an application to strike off and dissolve this company from ella.adams@example.com on 14 July 2026. We are currently processing the application. We'll accept or reject it within 2 working days.

If it's accepted, the company will typically be dissolved after a minimum period of 2 months, unless there are any objections. We are unable to confirm the exact date of dissolution.

Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors must send a copy of this application to all interested parties, within 7 days of making the application.

Download a copy of the application. Once we accept the application, we'll also publish a copy on the Companies House register where anyone can download it.

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to dissolve the company.

What happens next

If accepted, your request for the company to be struck off will be published as a notice in The Gazette.

If nobody objects, the company will be struck off the register shortly after the 2 months mentioned in the notice has passed.

A second notice will be published in the Gazette. This will mean the company does not legally exist any more.
