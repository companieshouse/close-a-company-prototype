The application to strike off and dissolve (close) this company has been accepted. The process to dissolve the company has now started.

Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors must send a copy of this application to all interested parties, within 7 days of making the application.

Download a copy of the application. You'll need to sign in with the same email address you used when applying. We've also published a copy in the company's filing history on the Companies House public register: https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/

Read the full list of interested parties you must tell about the company being dissolved: gov.uk/government/publications/striking-off-or-dissolving-a-limited-company/striking-off-or-dissolving-a-limited-company#how-to-apply-and-who-to-tell

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to dissolve the company.

What happens next

Your request for the company to be struck off will be published as a notice in The Gazette.

If nobody objects, the company will be struck off the register shortly after the 2 months mentioned in the notice has passed.

A second notice will be published in the Gazette. This will mean the company does not legally exist any more.
