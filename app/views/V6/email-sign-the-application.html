After you've signed

Once all directors have signed, sara.francis@example.com can log back in, pay the £13 fee and submit the application. They should notify you when they've done this.

Directors must then send a copy of the application to all interested parties within 7 days of making the application. You'll be able to download a copy by signing back in to the Apply to strike off and dissolve a company service, or by accessing the Companies House public register.
