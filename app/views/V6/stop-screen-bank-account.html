Access to company bank accounts and other assets

{% from "govuk/components/warning-text/macro.njk" import govukWarningText %} {% from "govuk/components/checkboxes/macro.njk" import govukCheckboxes %} {% from "govuk/components/button/macro.njk" import govukButton %}
{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You'll lose access to money in company bank accounts, and other company assets, once the company is dissolved.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

The directors or members should deal with all company assets before submitting this application. This includes, for example, money in company bank accounts and property owned by the company.

Once you've submitted this application and we've accepted it, the company will typically be dissolved (closed) after a minimum period of 2 months, unless there are any objections. We are unable to confirm the exact date of dissolution.

Any assets remaining in the company at the point of dissolution will automatically pass to the Crown. You'll need a court order to restore the company to recover them. This can be costly and time-consuming.

By selecting continue, you are confirming that you have read and understood this information.
