Application signed

The person who started the application should tell you when they have submitted it

We need to receive signatures from all signing directors. The person making the application can then pay for and submit it.

It may take some time for others to sign. You can check who has signed on this page.

Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors must send a copy of this application to all interested parties within 7 days of making the application.

When the person who started the application has submitted it, you'll be able to download a copy by signing back in to the Apply to strike off and dissolve a company service. Once we've accepted the application, we'll also publish a copy on the Companies House public register. This could take up to 2 working days from when it's submitted.

Read the full list of interested parties you must tell about the company closing (opens in a new tab).

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to close a company.
