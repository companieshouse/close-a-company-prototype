More information about who needs to sign

The application must be signed by more than half of the directors.

Directors who are people

Directors must sign the application themselves. Nobody can sign on their behalf.

Sometimes a director cannot sign. For example, because they no longer have mental capacity. If there are not enough directors who are able to sign, you'll need to make changes to the company directors (opens in new tab) before continuing with this application. This might include removing a person as a director or appointing a new director.

Corporate directors

For directors that are companies, an authorised person must sign on their behalf.
