Directors must inform all interested parties

The directors must send a copy of this application to all interested parties, within 7 days of making the application.

Download a copy of the application . Once we accept the application, we'll also publish a copy on the Companies House register where anyone can download it.

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to close the company.

What happens next

We'll publish a strike off notice for the company in the relevant Gazette. After 2 months, unless there is an objection to the company closing, another notice will be published in the relevant Gazette and the company will be dissolved.
