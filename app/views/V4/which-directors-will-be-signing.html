More information about who needs to sign
Directors who are people

Directors must sign the application themselves. Nobody can sign on their behalf.

Sometimes a director cannot sign. For example, because they no longer have mental capacity. If there are not enough directors who are able to sign, you'll need to make changes to the company directors (opens in new tab) before continuing with this application.

Corporate directors

For directors that are companies, you'll need to provide the name and email address of a person who the company has authorised to sign on its behalf.
