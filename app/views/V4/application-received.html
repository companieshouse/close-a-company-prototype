We'll email you within 2 working days to let you know if the application has been accepted or rejected.

Directors must inform all interested parties

Once we have accepted the application, the directors or members must send a copy to all interested parties within 7 days. To do this, they'll need to:

  • search for the company on the Companies House public register (opens in new tab)

  • select 'Filing history'
  • download the PDF document labelled 'Application to strike the company off the register' (this will only appear once we have accepted the application)

Read the full list of parties to tell about the company closing (opens in new tab) .

! Warning It is a criminal offence not to tell all interested parties that an application has been made to close the company.

What happens next

We'll publish a strike off notice for the company in the local Gazette. After 2 months, the notice will expire. In this time, if nobody has objected to the company closing, the company will be dissolved.
